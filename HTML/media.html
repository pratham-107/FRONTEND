HTML Media Tags Explained

<img> - Image

Displays an image on the webpage.

<audio> - Audio

Embeds audio content into the page, with controls to play, pause, and adjust volume.

<video> - Video

Embeds video content into the page, with controls to play, pause, and adjust volume.

<iframe> - Inline Frame

Embeds content from another webpage into the current webpage.

<source> - Media Source

Specifies multiple media resources for the <video> and <audio> elements.
